Introduction

This web application was developed as a resource to aide in the creation and upkeep of a star wars saga edition character.

If you hit the "Create a New Hero" button then you will see a page that will allow you to create a new non-droid character with all stats starting at level one.

The next button labeled "Modifiy an Existing Hero" will take you to a page that will provide you with access to all your characters that you have saved where you can view their stats and/or update them.

This app utilizes the information that can be found on the Star Wars Saga Edition Wiki to help determine what is permissable when creating or updating a character.
